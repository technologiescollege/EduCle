Limitations

Pepakura Designer is a shareware program. A few of its functions are unavailable until you enter a password. The password to enable the unavailable functions is provided when you pay for it.
The unavailable functions are the "Saving" and "Exporting" functions. Except for these functions, you can use all functions without any limitations even before purchasing a password.
If you like Pepakura Designer and want to use these unavailable functions, please buy a password after you confirmed that Pepakura Designer runs on your PC without troubles.
